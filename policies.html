Agri-Drone Policies

PTO (Paid Time Off) Policy

  • All full-time employees receive 20 paid days off per year.
  • Sick days are flexible—take them as needed, no doctor's note required.
  • National holidays are observed based on your location.
  • No approval bottlenecks: simply block your time on the shared calendar and notify your manager.
  • We encourage everyone to take at least 5 consecutive days off annually to recharge.

Company Values (Employee Value Proposition)

At Agri-Drone, we build the future by empowering each individual to experiment boldly, learn constantly, and balance ambition with well-being.
  • Autonomy with Accountability: Own your work, be transparent.
  • Bias for Action: Speed wins, but so does feedback.
  • Human-Centric AI: We solve for people, not just metrics.
  • Radical Clarity: Simplicity in communication = speed in execution.
  • Wellness Wins: Burnout is not a badge of honor.

Remote Work Policy

  • We are fully remote by default, with optional monthly team meetups.
  • Core hours: 11AM–4PM IST for synchronous collaboration.
  • Async-first mindset: meetings only when necessary.
  • Internet stipend provided (₹2000/month).
  • Use Slack status to communicate your availability.
