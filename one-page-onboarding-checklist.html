One-Page Onboarding Checklist

Before Day 1

  • Offer letter signed
  • NDA and legal paperwork completed
  • Email, Slack, and tool accounts provisioned
  • Welcome email from founder or HR
  • Workspace (remote setup or office desk) arranged

Day 1

  • Welcome message from team (Slack/email)
  • 30-min onboarding call with People Ops
  • Overview of company mission, vision, values
  • Intro to key tools (email, Slack, Notion, HRIS)
  • Org chart walkthrough (who does what)
  • Assigned buddy or mentor
  • Team introduction (async video or Zoom)
  • Setup payroll, PTO, benefits accounts
  • First mini-project assigned
